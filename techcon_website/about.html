Our History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts to a world-renowned conference that attracts thousands of attendees each year. It has served as a platform for launching groundbreaking innovations and fostering collaboration across industries.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to inspire innovation, promote knowledge sharing, and empower individuals to shape the future of technology responsibly and inclusively.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds in the tech industry, who have shared their insights and inspired generations of innovators.

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Widely regarded as the first computer programmer, Dr. Lovelace’s visionary ideas have influenced modern computing. At TechCon, she inspired audiences by highlighting the importance of creativity in technology.

Alan Turing

Known as the father of modern computing, Alan Turing’s groundbreaking work on algorithms and artificial intelligence has left a lasting impact on technology and society.
